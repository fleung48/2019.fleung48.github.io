About me

I'm a rising senior at Monta Vista High school, with interests in computational finance, digital engineering and code evangelism.

Outside of school, I'm heavily immersed within the Bay Area CS community, leading and creating events for Bay Area high school students to attend. In addition, I am currently involved with three national nonprofits that provide opportunities to underrepresented groups in tech for them to gain exposure in computer science.

This website gives a general overview about me and what I've accomplished. You can find out more about my specific positions and accomplishments on my LinkedIn profile.

Projects

2019: Smart Lamp with IoT Technology- A fully customizable neopixel ring lamp powered by a NodeMcu ESP8266 that senses temperature, movement, and light to activate with embedded IoT technology and a security mode to prevent home invasions.
